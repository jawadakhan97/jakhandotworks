Synd Featured Project

The City of Synd was once a beaming port city, an ancient metropolis, but now it's fallen into ruin. Where it led culturally, technologically, and artistically it now falters behind other cities. However, someone is trying to rebuild, and the mystery of who and why grips the city.

Key Features

  • Explore the city of Synd, a former metropolis of the pre-modern world
  • Investigate, discover the secret of what happened to the city
